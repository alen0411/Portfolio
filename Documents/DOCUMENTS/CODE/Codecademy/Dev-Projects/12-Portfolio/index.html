About Me

Last year I decided to make a big change and transition my carrer into a Front End Web Developer. I started my research online, from viewing tutorials on YouTube to lectures like CS50, which inspired me even more on my new path. Shortly after, I had enrolled in an online Codecademy FE Developer course to obtain a deeper knowledge and overview of my new field and future profession.

Being halfway through my course, I am enjoying every Bit of it and I am looking forward to completing it and soon start working as a Front End Developer.
